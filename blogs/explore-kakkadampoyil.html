Explore Kakkadampoyil:
The Gavi of Malabar

Discover the hidden gem of the Western Ghats, where mist meets mystery.

"On the heights of the Western Ghats bordering Kerala, Kakkadampoyil is a hill station surrounded by dense forests, misty mountains, foggy hill ranges, and beautiful waterfalls."

Known fondly as the "Gavi of Malabar," this serene destination is celebrated for its lovely breeze and cool climate that attracts tourists from across the globe. It serves as a natural abode for those seeking refuge from the city heat. At Clouds End Resort, we provide the perfect vantage point to experience this magical beauty and feel the deep serenity of nature.

A Wanderlust's Paradise

For the true explorer, Kakkadampoyil offers a treasure trove of attractions ranging from sparkling lakes and thundering waterfalls to dense woodlands. As you traverse the mountain ranges, you are welcomed by streams flowing freely through the lush greenery, creating a symphony of nature that soothes the soul.

Kozhippara Waterfalls

One of the most significant spots near Kakkadampoyil is the Kozhippara Waterfall. It is a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike, offering activities such as trekking and rock climbing. Surrounded by fascinating forest greenery, this exotic waterfall presents an enchanting view that instantly relaxes the mind.

Valamthode Waterfalls

Situated amidst lush greenery, the Valamthode Waterfall is another captivating attraction nearby. It offers a refreshing experience for travelers looking to dip their toes in pristine mountain water and enjoy a picnic in the wild.

Major Attractions Checklist

While you stay with us at Clouds End Resort, make sure to check these spots off your list:

  • Kozhippara Waterfalls: The jewel of the region.
  • Nedumkayam Rainforest: A dense forest area perfect for nature walks.
  • Pazhassiraja Tomb: A historical site of great significance.
  • Teak Museum: Learn about the history of the world-famous Nilambur Teak.
  • Thusharagiri & Arippara Waterfalls: More cascading beauties to explore.
  • Kakkayam Dam: A scenic reservoir ideal for boating and photography.
